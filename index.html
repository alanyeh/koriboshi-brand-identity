01
Who We Are
Koriboshi brings Japanese craft to active sauna culture. Born from climbing gyms and inspired by Japanese cotton hats, we create functional gear for people who build daily rituals around movement and recovery.

Active Ritual

Sauna as daily practice, not luxury spa. Post-climb, post-run, post-training. We're for athletes, movers, and ritual-builders who sauna to recover and reset.

Japanese Function

Inspired by Japanese design philosophy: simple, durable, thoughtful. Soft cotton that breathes. Construction that lasts. Function over decoration.

Honest Quality

No marketing fluff. No wellness clichés. Just well-made cotton hats that do their job: protect your head, enhance your session, last through daily use.

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of one letter around the logo. Never crowd it with other elements.

Minimum Size

Logo should never appear smaller than 120px wide in digital applications, or 1.5 inches in print.

Usage

Use black on light backgrounds, white on dark backgrounds. Never distort, rotate, or apply effects to the logo.

85/15 Rule

Neutrals should comprise 85% of any composition. Accent colors are used deliberately for borders, highlights, and calls-to-action.

Forest Green

Our primary accent color, pulled directly from our Forest Green hat. Use for borders, section dividers, hover states, and primary CTAs. Grounding and natural.

Product Harmony

All accent colors come from our actual product line. This creates visual harmony between the brand and the hats themselves. Use bronze rust for warmth, dusty rose for softer moments.

Hierarchy

Create hierarchy through size and weight, not color. Headlines 48-96px, subheads 24-32px, body 15-18px, captions 12-14px.

Line Height

Display: 1.1-1.2 (tight and impactful). Body: 1.6-1.8 (readable and breathable). More space makes text easier to read.

Letter Spacing

Large display: -1px to -2px (tighten). Small caps/labels: +1px to +3px (open up). Body text: default tracking.

05
Photography
Real moments, natural light, authentic context. Show sauna as part of active life— post-workout, in gym settings, daily ritual. Never overly styled or spa-like.
DO THIS

Perfect Product Photography

Clean neutral background, soft even lighting, subtle shadow for depth. Product centered and clearly visible. Shows texture and craftsmanship. This is exactly the style to follow.

AVOID THIS

Generic Luxury

Overly styled spa settings, posed perfection, ultra-clean commercial staging, generic wellness stock photography. Busy backgrounds or harsh lighting.

Product Shots

Clean neutral backgrounds (light gray, off-white, cream). Soft, even lighting with subtle shadows. Product centered, clearly visible. Show texture and craftsmanship. See example above—this is the standard.

Lifestyle

Climbing gym saunas, athletic settings, real people in authentic moments. Show the context—training gear nearby, water bottles, post-workout environment. Natural light, honest composition.

Lighting & Shadows

Soft, diffused natural light. Subtle shadows add depth without drama. No harsh directional light or high contrast. The shadow should ground the product, not distract from it.

07
Voice & Messaging
Direct, confident, unpretentious. We explain clearly without marketing hype. Active voice, concrete details, no wellness clichés.

✓ Do This

"Protects your head from extreme heat so you can sauna longer and more comfortably."
Direct benefit. Active voice. Clear and specific.

✗ Not This

"Experience enhanced thermal comfort with our revolutionary heat-protective headwear solution."
Too corporate. Vague marketing speak. Sounds like everyone else.

✓ Do This

"Double-layered cotton. Soft, breathable, machine washable. Built for daily use."
Specific materials. Short sentences. Concrete benefits.

✗ Not This

"Crafted from premium sustainable fibers to elevate your wellness journey."
Wellness cliché. Vague claims. Sounds pretentious.

Be Direct

"Protects your head" not "Provides protection for." "Built for daily use" not "Designed to be used daily." Active voice, clear subject.

Be Specific

"Double-layered cotton" not "Premium materials." "Machine washable" not "Easy care." Concrete details build trust.

Be Real

"Born from climbing gyms" not "Inspired by wellness culture." Show origin, admit problems we solved, speak to real use.
